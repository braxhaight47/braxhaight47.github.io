Magic the Gathering is a trading card game that is played by millions around the world. The game focuses on two to four players, depending in the game mode, and players will fight until the life points of their oppenents hit zero. This is done by summoning creatures, playing sorceries, casting instants, and a multitude of other cards that fit your stradegy. In Magic the Gathering, there are five colors, these colors dennote different factions and by combining the different colors you create a unique stradegy. Each page on this site will go over the general concept of each color and some personalities that match them as well to help you find your color!
